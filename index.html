About Me

Hello! I'm Renan Marcelo, unique with just one surname, no compound names here. Hailing from the vibrant city of Sorocaba, São Paulo, I am a proud graduate of FACENS. With 6 years of experience in game development, my journey has been diverse, spanning Windows and Mobile games, with a keen focus on Virtual Reality. As a seasoned professional, I've evolved from a Junior Game Developer to a Senior Lead Game Developer. My expertise lies in Unity 3D, and I'm now seeking opportunities in the gaming industry where I can showcase my skills and continue to grow. I'm ready to take on new challenges and contribute to exciting projects!
